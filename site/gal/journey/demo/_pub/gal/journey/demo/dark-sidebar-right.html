ABOUT SOPU QI

I'm Sopu Qi, and I'm fascinated by the idea of owning my own place in the country. I grew up in a rural home. I tried Seattle and didn't like it much, but I like that part of the country. It's my goal to live in the country with at least 2 chickens and maybe a couple capybaras. Since I don't want to make too many mistakes with the biggest purchase of my life I'm keeping track of the things I've learned about buying rural land, mobile homes, modular homes, tiny homes, and other ways to keep from paying $1 million for my own home in the Seattle area.
